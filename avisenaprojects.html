TrueCare2U App

Objective

TrueCare2U App is a digital platform with easy & convenient access to healthcare providers and medical treatments from the comforts of your own home.


Features

Appointment Booking: Book for appointment at any time of the day

Care Options: Choice of care via home visit of tele-consult

Booking Convenience: Convenience of choosing time & date according to your own schedule

Cashless Payment: Pay on the app via online payment gateway.

Record of Visit: Receive “visit summary” after every care visit on the app.

Reminders & Notifications: Appointment notification reminders so you won’t miss any appointment.

Secure Authentication: Multi-layer security for protecting sensitive health data.

AviHealth - Patient App

Objective

To digitize patient experience in healthcare for booking appointments and knowing Avisena.


Features

App Register: Users can only register if they have previously visited Avisena and registered at Avisena using the email that was provided.

Appointment Booking: Easy scheduling with preferred doctors and available time slots.

Medical History: Complete access to personal medical records and treatment history.

Push Notifications: Real-time updates on appointments, reminders, and health alerts.

Multi-language Support: Interface available in multiple languages for diverse healthcare settings.


*App expected to go live mid October 2025




Avisena Doctor App

Objective

To provide doctors with comprehensive tools to view census and statistics, monitor admitted patients, and manage patient information efficiently.


Features

Census & Statistics: View hospital census data and statistical reports for better patient management.

Admitted Patients: Monitor patients who are currently admitted with real-time status updates.

Patient Notes: Access and manage patient notes for procedures and treatments.

OT Management: View and manage Operating Theatre schedules and patient procedures.

Financial Fees: Access patient financial information and medical fees.

New Patients: View and manage newly registered patients in the system.

Appointment List: View patient appointment lists organized by date for better scheduling.


*App expected to go live mid October 2025
