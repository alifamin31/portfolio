Caricarz

Objective

Online Car Shopping Platform: Explore a curated list of new, used, and recon cars from various brands.


Features

Keep Login: Users remain logged in until they choose to log out, eliminating the need to repeatedly go through the login page.

Car Listings: Browse a wide selection of cars available for users to choose from.

Filtering Options: Users can filter the list based on their preferences.

Priority Listings: Hot or premium cars are always prioritized and displayed at the top of the list.
